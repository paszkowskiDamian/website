Ridgeline is where the two halves of my work meet: a dashboard for trail and elevation analytics that started because I wanted better maps of the routes I was photographing.

## Charts that feel like terrain

The charts had to feel like the thing they described. Elevation profiles render as layered ridgeline plots — the visualization the product is named for — and the palette comes straight from the landscape: fog, stone, and late light.

## Where it landed

Ridgeline was acquired by a mapping company in 2024. The ridgeline chart lives on in their app.
